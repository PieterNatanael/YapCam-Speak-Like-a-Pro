# Privacy Policy

YapCam: Speak Like a Pro
Last updated: April 11, 2026

## Overview

YapCam: Speak Like a Pro ("the App") is a teleprompter and video recording app. 
We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the 
App accesses and how it is used.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The App has no 
servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

## Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions solely to provide core functionality:

**Camera**  
Used to record video while you read your script. Video is saved locally on your 
device only. We never access or transmit your camera feed.

**Microphone**  
Used to capture audio during video recording. Audio is saved locally on your 
device as part of the recorded video file. We never access or transmit your 
microphone input.

**Photo Library**  
Used only when you explicitly tap "Save to Photos" to save a recorded video to 
your Camera Roll. We do not read from your photo library.

## Local Storage

All recorded videos and scripts are stored locally on your device using 
FileManager. No content leaves your device unless you choose to share it 
yourself via the system share sheet.

## Subscriptions

YapCam offers an auto-renewable subscription ($9/year) processed entirely by 
Apple through the App Store. We do not see or store your payment information. 
Apple's privacy policy governs subscription and payment data:  
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. It 
contains no user accounts, no messaging, and no data collection of any kind.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a 
new "Last updated" date.
