# Privacy Policy

YapCam: Say It On Camera
Last updated: April 15, 2026

## Overview

YapCam: Say It On Camera ("the App") is a teleprompter and video recording app.
We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

* No user accounts
* No analytics or tracking
* No third-party SDKs
* No data is sent to any servers

All data created in the App remains on your device.

## Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

**Camera**
Used to record videos while you read your script. Video is recorded only when you explicitly start recording and is stored locally on your device. We do not access or transmit camera data.

**Microphone**
Used to capture audio during video recording. Audio is recorded only when you explicitly start recording and is stored locally as part of your video. We do not access or transmit microphone data.

**Photos and Videos / Photo Library**
Used only when you choose to save or access media. For example, when you tap "Save to Photos", the App saves your recorded video to your device’s media library. The App does not access, scan, or collect your existing photos or videos.

## Local Storage

All videos, scripts, and related content are stored locally on your device.
No content leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it using your device’s system sharing features.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Subscriptions

YapCam may offer optional paid features or subscriptions. All payments are processed by the platform provider (such as Apple App Store or Google Play).

We do not have access to your payment details. Payment processing and related data are handled according to the platform provider’s privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not include accounts, social features, or data collection.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
